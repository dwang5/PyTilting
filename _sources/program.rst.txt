Program
****************************
The program has three parts.

Puc
---
The Puc class is used to construct primitive unit cell, and define the basic distortion operations 
on the primivite unit cell level.

   :members:

Distortion
-----------
Build a supercell and generate the desired structures in the reciprocal space based on the transformed information.

Decode
-----------
